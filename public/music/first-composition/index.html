Autumn Leaves - Jazz Piano Cover

March 20, 2024
Genre: Jazz

This is my interpretation of the classic jazz standard “Autumn Leaves”. I wanted to explore different harmonic approaches while maintaining the melancholic mood of the piece.

About the Performance

I recorded this using a Yamaha grand piano, focusing on:

  • Rich harmonic voicings
  • Subtle rhythmic variations
  • Dynamic control

Technical Details

  • Recorded: March 2024
  • Piano: Yamaha C7
  • Key: E minor
  • Tempo: ♩ = 92

The arrangement incorporates some elements from Bill Evans’ interpretation while adding my own harmonic ideas in the bridge section.
